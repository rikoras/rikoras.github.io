# 请别在意，我是旅人
# 要继续旅行才行
* * *

## 2.18

```
Its your consideration, your patient, dedication, devotion, action, forbearance that keep me flying high.
是你的体谅，你的耐心，你的牺牲，你所挚爱容忍的一切送我上高空翱翔。

Compassion, reinforcement, encouragement, conviction, you are the star in my eyes.
指引我向前，期许和坚定，你是我的夜航星。

Acceptance, recognition,assurance, absolution, even your existence takes the burden of my mind.
接受和认可，担保和赦免，你的存在本身就卸下了我心中的千钧重负。

Allow me to fight by your side.
请允许我同你一同战斗。
```
